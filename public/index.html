Game Rules

1. Each player has 15 monsters to place on the board.
2. The game is played in turns. On each turn, a player can add, move, or skip.
3. Combat rules:
  - Vampire vs Werewolf: Werewolf is removed.
  - Werewolf vs Ghost: Ghost is removed.
  - Ghost vs Vampire: Vampire is removed.
  - Same type: Both are removed.
4. A player is eliminated if they lose 10 monsters or run out of monsters to place.
5. The last remaining player wins the game.
